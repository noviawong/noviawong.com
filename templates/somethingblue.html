Something Blue Consulting: Improving Communications Between Organizations

Role: Project Manager

Skills Used: Contextual Inquiries, Literature Reviews, Affinity Walls, Public Speaking, Google Drive

Summary
Something Blue Consulting was a team-based course project. We were given three months to work on this project with a client. Our client approached us with a desire to update and increase their social media usage. After coducting six different contextual inquiries, my team and I organized the interview findings and our background research information, we put together an affinity wall. Through this analysis, we further identified additional issues that the organization was facing and brainstormed solutions around these newly identified issues. In the end, we suggested two solutions, one being the most practical and another being the ideal solution for the orgnaization. Given the nature of the organization, both solutions consisted of minimal technology usage, and instead focused on changing the way information was communicated between our client and another organization on campus.
