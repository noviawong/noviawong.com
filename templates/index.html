Project Highlights

MealSpace is a team-based self-initiated project for 2018 CHI Student Design Competition. It is a solution aimed to solve the problem of college students lacking healthy eating habits. The process of user research and design iteration took place over the course of 4 months.

Something Blue Consulting was a course project at the University of Michigan, where I worked in a team of 4 to assess existing problems of our client, develop interview protocols and conduct interviews with stakeholders, as well as analyze interview results using affinity wall.

Inspired by the flaws and constant frustrations of some of the modern account authentication systems, this study explored users' problems with password management and usage. A final written report detailed study findings and result analysis.
